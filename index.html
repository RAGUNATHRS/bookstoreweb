RICH DAD POOR DAD

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" is a bestselling personal finance book written by Robert T. Kiyosaki. Published in 1997, the book challenges conventional beliefs about money and provides readers with valuable insights into building wealth and achieving financial independence. Set against the backdrop of the author's own life experiences, the book contrasts the financial teachings of two father figures: his biological father (referred to as "Poor Dad") and the father of his best friend (referred to as "Rich Dad"). Through engaging anecdotes and practical lessons, Kiyosaki explores the differences in mindset, attitudes, and financial strategies that set these two men apart. The book's core message revolves around the importance of financial education, investing wisely, and embracing entrepreneurial thinking. "Rich Dad Poor Dad" has had a profound impact on how people approach money, sparking conversations about financial literacy, wealth-building, and the pursuit of financial freedom.
